# Privacy Policy for Ref Tracking

**Effective date:** February 13, 2026

Ref Tracking ("the App") is developed by Ulysse van Duinen ("we", "us", "our").
This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the App.

## 1. Data We Collect

We do **not** collect personal data on our own servers.

Data you create in the App may include:

- Reference entries (for example: titles, names, notes, tags, categories, and custom fields)
- Images you choose to add
- App settings and list configuration

## 2. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on your device and, if enabled on your device, may sync via Apple iCloud (CloudDocuments) associated with your Apple ID.

We do not operate a backend database for user content.

## 3. Widget Data

If you use the app widget, a subset of your reference data is copied into Apple's App Group shared container on your device so the widget can display content. This data remains on-device within Apple's app sandbox system.

## 4. Photos and Camera Access

If you choose to add images, the App may request access to:

- Photo Library (to pick images)
- Camera (to capture images)

These permissions are optional and only used for features you explicitly invoke.

## 5. File Import/Export

The App supports importing/exporting your content (for example CSV/ZIP). Files are read from and written to locations you choose through Apple's system file picker.

## 6. Analytics, Tracking, Ads

- No third-party analytics SDKs are used by us for user tracking.
- No advertising SDKs are used.
- We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

## 7. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.
Data may be processed by Apple services you use (such as iCloud) under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains until you delete it.
You can delete data by:

- Deleting references/lists in the App
- Removing the App from your device
- Removing associated app data from iCloud (if synced)

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed specifically to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children on our own servers.

## 10. Security

We rely on Apple platform security, sandboxing, and local/iCloud storage protections. No method of storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published with a new effective date.
